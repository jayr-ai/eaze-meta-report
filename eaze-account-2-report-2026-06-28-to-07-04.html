Overview00

Executive summary

Across seven days (28 Jun–4 Jul) the account spent $4,177 in total. Of that, $4,167 ran through the four substantive campaigns and about $10 was residual on two near-zero legacy campaigns still carrying old pixel tags. Those campaigns produced 70 booked appointments and 29 website leads.

  • Best appointment engine: Medspa UGC Reels CBO at $27.85 per appointment, 49 of the account's 70 appointments, and almost entirely one ad, UGC Reel Ad 05, which alone carries 47 of the campaign's 49 appointments on $1,237.53 spend at $26.33 each.
  • Best lead engine: RPF 01 Dentists at $13.46 per lead and a 5.43% CTR on $349.93 spend. Meta's opportunity score tool separately flags this ad set to scale, estimating 74% more conversions at a higher budget.
  • Biggest drain: the Med Spa ABO Broad campaign, the largest spender at $2,136, at $101.73 per appointment — roughly 3.7x Medspa UGC Reels. Two of its ads stand out as costly: V1 AI Image 5 Med Spa ($412.86 for 3 appointments, $137.62 each) and a second ad named V1 Video 2 Med Spa ($107.08 for 1 appointment, despite a striking 9.38% CTR).
  • The hero ad, verified: UGC Reel Ad 05 opened at a real 4.36% CTR on day one, then settled into a steady 2.39% to 2.87% band for the remaining six days while spend scaled up to $1,238 total. That is a plateau, not a collapse.

Data & actions12

Action list

  1. Scale RPF 01 Dentists. It is the most efficient engine on the account at $13.46 per lead and a 5.43% CTR on $350 spend, and Meta itself flags it to scale with an estimated 74% more conversions.
  2. Keep pushing UGC Reel Ad 05 — do not panic-refresh it. It opened at 4.36% CTR and settled into a steady 2.39% to 2.87% band for the rest of the week while carrying $1,238 spend at $26.33 per appointment. That is a plateau, not a fatigue collapse.
  3. Trim the weakest Med Spa image ads. V1 AI Image 5 ($413 for 3 appointments, $138 each) and V1 Video 2 (b) — a striking 9.38% CTR but only 1 appointment on $107 — are the drains dragging Med Spa ABO Broad to $101.73 per appointment.
  4. Watch V1 AI Image 2 Dentist under scale. It spent $449 this week at $64.19 per appointment with a 4.18% CTR — solid, but worth a check-in before pushing further budget.
  5. Exclude the 21:00 to 22:00 hours. 0.99% and 1.34% CTR — the weakest block on the account by a clear margin.
  6. Shift weight toward Android. Android phone clicks cost $1.83 against iPhone's $5.45 — almost 3x cheaper — while iPhone still eats 73% of spend.
  7. Take the free Meta wins. Let Meta add music to the 3 flagged ads (+2 points) and add a 9:16 vertical video with audio to Reels placements (+1 point) — no extra spend required.
  8. Confirm the Derma campaign's trajectory. Still the most expensive lead source at $105.30 per lead on 3 leads from $316 spend — roughly 7.8x the cost of a Dentist lead.

Scale the Dentist engine, hold the hero ad steady, trim the identified image-ad drains, and the account is in strong shape.
