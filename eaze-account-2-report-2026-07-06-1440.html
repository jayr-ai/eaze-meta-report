Executive summary

Over the first two days of the week (Sun–Mon, July 5–6), Eaze Account 2 spent $1,063.45 across three active campaigns, generating 23 med spa appointments at a blended $40.54 each and 8 dental leads at $16.39 each. Instagram absorbed 56% of the budget but ran at roughly half Facebook's CTR and more than double its CPC, and the iPhone/Instagram combination is soaking up most of the spend at a real efficiency discount versus Android and Facebook. Both flagship appointment ads (UGC Reel Ad 05 and V1 | AI Image 2 | Dentist) posted a lower CTR on their second day than their first, but with only two days on record this is too early to call fatigue rather than normal week-open variance — it needs a longer window to confirm.

  • Best appointment engine: Aashish Maru UGC Reels (CBO, Demographic) at $31.63 per appointment, 11 appointments — 35% cheaper per result than the ABO Med Spa + Dental campaign.
  • Best lead engine: RPF 01 - Dentists at $16.39 per lead, 8 leads — the account's only lead-generating campaign this window and its most efficient result type overall.
  • Biggest drain: five near-zero orphan ads (UGC Reel Ad 03, V1 | AI Image 1 | Dentist, V1 | Image 1 | Med Spa, V1 | AI Image 2 | Med Spa, UGC Reel Ad 04) — $15.59 combined spend, zero results across two days.
  • Notable change: the account's opportunity score dropped from 88 (this morning's ~4:41 AM PDT pull, same week-to-date window) to 81 now — a real 7-point move; spend/appointment/lead growth this run is just later-day Monday delivery, not a new period.

Action list

  1. Shift incremental budget toward Aashish Maru UGC Reels. Same appointment result type as the ABO Med Spa + Dental campaign, at $31.63/appointment vs $48.70/appointment — 35% cheaper.
  2. Apply Meta's A+ Creative enhancements bundle. The single highest-leverage recommendation available (7-point opportunity score lift, 3% lower cost per result) and it already covers nearly every active ad.
  3. Spot-check why the opportunity score dropped from 88 to 81 today. The A+ Creative bundle (7 points) newly appeared between this morning's pull and this one — confirm in Ads Manager whether that's a genuinely new opportunity or a scoring recalculation, since the two would call for different responses.
  4. Hold off on judging creative fatigue. UGC Reel Ad 05 (2.51%→1.67% CTR) and V1 | AI Image 2 | Dentist (2.93%→2.45% CTR) both softened day-over-day, but with only 2 days of data this could be normal week-open pacing. Re-check after a full 7-day window.
  5. Rebalance platform delivery away from Instagram feed. Facebook runs 3.57% CTR / $3.47 CPC vs Instagram's 1.99% CTR / $8.08 CPC, yet Instagram holds 56% of spend.
  6. Test weighting delivery toward Android smartphone. 3.86% CTR at $2.83 CPC vs iPhone's 2.49% CTR at $6.04 CPC, while iPhone still absorbs 77% of spend.
  7. Pause or refresh the five orphan ads. UGC Reel Ad 03, V1 | AI Image 1 | Dentist, V1 | Image 1 | Med Spa, V1 | AI Image 2 | Med Spa, UGC Reel Ad 04 — $15.59 combined, zero results across two days.
