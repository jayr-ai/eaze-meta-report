Executive summary

Over the three days of real data in this window (Sun Jul 5 – Tue Jul 7), the account spent $1,658.30 across three active campaigns, producing 33 appointments (med spa) at a blended $44.70 each and 10 leads (dental/derma) at $18.32 each. Spend is concentrated in two appointment campaigns — "AI Funding Solutions | Med Spa + Dental | ABO | Broad" ($904.44) and "Aashish Maru | Medspa UGC Reels" ($570.70) — with the single lead campaign, "RPF 01 - Dentists - WV - Images," running steady at $183.16. Instagram absorbed 56% of spend but at nearly double Facebook's CPC ($8.90 vs $3.67). Six ads spent a combined $36.80 with zero verified results and are candidates to pause or refresh.

  • Best appointment engine: "Aashish Maru | Medspa UGC Reels" at $31.71/appointment (18 results, $570.70 spend), carried almost entirely by "UGC Reel Ad 05" ($30.04/appointment on $540.64).
  • Best lead engine: "RPF 01 - Dentists - WV - Images" at $18.32/lead — the account's only lead campaign, so there is no internal comparison to rank it against.
  • Biggest drain: Six ads ("UGC Reel Ad 03," "V1 | Image 1 | Med Spa," "V1 | AI Image 2 | Med Spa," "V1 | Carousel | Image 1 | Med Spa," "UGC Reel Ad 01," "UGC Reel Ad 04") spent $36.80 combined with zero results.
  • Notable change: Opportunity score climbed 3 points (81 → 84) since the last tracked run; no campaigns were paused or launched.

Action list

  1. Pause or refresh the 6 zero-result ads ("UGC Reel Ad 03," "V1 | Image 1 | Med Spa," "V1 | AI Image 2 | Med Spa," "V1 | Carousel | Image 1 | Med Spa," "UGC Reel Ad 01," "UGC Reel Ad 04") — $36.80 combined spend, 0 verified results this window.
  2. Investigate "V1 | Image 4 | Med Spa" at $97.75/appointment, the worst cost-per-result among ads with meaningful spend ($293.26) — swap creative or reduce budget.
  3. Consider a small budget test on "V1 | AI Image 1 | Dentist" — $4.09/appointment on only $12.27 spend and 3 results is a strong early signal, but the sample is too small to scale on yet.
  4. Shift discretionary budget from Instagram toward Facebook where CPC is $3.67 vs Instagram's $8.90 for similar creative — Instagram still carries 56% of spend despite the gap.
  5. Re-evaluate the "[Do Not Touch]" label on "Aashish Maru | Medspa UGC Reels" — it is currently the best appointment engine in the account ($31.71/appointment); confirm with the team whether that label still reflects intent or is stale.
  6. Apply Meta's standard creative-enhancements bundle across the 10 flagged ads for an estimated 3% lower cost per result (+4 opportunity-score points) — the single highest-value recommendation available.
  7. Enable auto-music on the 3 flagged low-spend ads for an estimated 5% lower cost per result (+2 points) — low effort given these ads already have negligible spend.
  8. Protect the noon–1PM dayparting window and scrutinize 10–11PM spend — noon is the best-value hour on both CTR and CPC (5.14% / $2.71), while 10–11PM is the worst on both (0.96% / $14.30); consider a dayparting exclusion for that hour if the platform allows it.

Net direction: the account is healthy and slightly improving (opportunity score +3 points), carried by two appointment campaigns and one steady lead campaign, with a small, identifiable pocket of dead spend ($36.80 across 6 ads) and a clear platform-efficiency gap (Instagram vs. Facebook) worth addressing before scaling further.
