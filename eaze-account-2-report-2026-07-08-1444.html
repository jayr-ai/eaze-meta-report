Executive summary

Across the window (3 full settled days, Sun Jul 5 – Tue Jul 7, plus roughly 14.7 hours of Wed Jul 8 on the advertiser's PST8PDT clock at pull time), Eaze Account 2 spent $2,545.55 to generate 51 appointments and 12 leads. The Aashish Maru UGC Reels campaign remains the most efficient appointment engine at $38.07 per appointment, well ahead of the broader Med Spa + Dental ABO campaign at $51.06 per appointment. RPF 01 stays the account's only lead-generating campaign, converting at $18.01 per lead on a 4.58% CTR — still the single cheapest result type in the account. Instagram again absorbed the majority of spend (57.4%, $1,462.44) at the account's weakest CTR (2.13%) and highest CPC ($7.62) among the major platforms, while Facebook ran at half that CPC. The opportunity score slipped again, from 82 to 79 — its second consecutive drop — as several Meta recommendations (extra Reels-video and campaign-scaling items) gained additional unclaimed lift points this run.

  • Best appointment engine: Aashish Maru [Do Not Touch] | Medspa UGC Reels — $38.07 per appointment, 21 appointments, 1.90% CTR.
  • Best lead engine: RPF 01 - Dentists - WV - Images — $18.01 per lead, 12 leads, 4.58% CTR (the account's only lead-generating campaign this window).
  • Biggest drain: Instagram as a platform — $1,462.44 (57.4% of spend) at $7.62 CPC and 2.13% CTR, the account's least efficient major channel.
  • Notable change: Opportunity score fell a second time, 82 → 79, since this morning's report; "V1 | Image 4 | Med Spa" reversed its 3-day CTR decline (up to 2.48% today), while the "Eaze Consulting" pixel's Lead match regression (6.1, no PII keys) held steady, confirming it as an ongoing issue rather than a one-off reading.
