Executive summary

Today (Sun Jul 12, roughly 16 hours old on the advertiser's PST8PDT clock at pull time — the first day of a new week-to-date window), Eaze Account 2 spent $636.74 to generate 10 appointments and 5 leads. Only three campaigns show spend so far: the "RELAUNCH" Med Spa + Dental campaign ($327.49, 6 appointments, $54.58/appt), the original Aashish Maru UGC Reels campaign ($261.75, 4 appointments, $65.44/appt), and RPF 01 ($47.50, 5 leads, $9.50/lead). Notably, RELAUNCH is outperforming the original Aashish Maru campaign on cost per appointment today — a reversal from its more expensive showing across all of last week. Instagram again took the majority of spend (62.0%, $394.62) at the account's weakest CTR (1.36%) and highest CPC ($10.96). The opportunity score fell again, from 87 to 77, driven mainly by a new 11-point "fragmentation" recommendation on two similar RELAUNCH ad sets. Separately, the "Eaze Consulting" pixel's Lead match quality — flagged as a confirmed, persistent regression across the last three reports — now shows a strong recovery to 9.2 with full PII coverage, worth confirming holds on the next pull.

  • Best appointment engine (today): RELAUNCH | AI Funding Solutions | Med Spa + Dental — $54.58 per appointment, 6 appointments, 1.73% CTR.
  • Best lead engine: RPF 01 - Dentists - WV - Images — $9.50 per lead, 5 leads, 4.31% CTR — the cheapest single-day lead cost tracked yet for this campaign.
  • Biggest drain: Instagram as a platform — $394.62 (62.0% of spend) at $10.96 CPC and 1.36% CTR, the account's least efficient major channel today.
  • Notable change: Opportunity score fell 87 → 77 on a new 11-point fragmentation flag; the "Eaze Consulting" pixel's Lead match quality appears to have recovered from a 3-report-confirmed regression (6.1 → 9.2) — see Sections 10–11.
