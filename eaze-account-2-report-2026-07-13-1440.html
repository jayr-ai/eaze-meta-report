Executive summary

Across the first two days of the new Sun–Sat week (Sunday complete, Monday ~14.7 hours in at pull time), Eaze Account 2 has spent $1,410.42 to generate 20 appointments and 9 leads. RPF 01 remains the account's clear efficiency standout at $11.80/lead. On the appointment side the ranking has flipped from last report: RELAUNCH is now the cheaper engine at $57.02/appointment (13 appointments) while Aashish Maru [Do Not Touch] has slipped to $80.44/appointment (7 appointments) as its flagship ad kept spending Monday with no new bookings yet. The most significant change this pull is the opportunity score's rebound, 71 → 76, alongside a new five-ad "System Focused" creative cohort that just entered the RELAUNCH campaign and is still too fresh to judge.
