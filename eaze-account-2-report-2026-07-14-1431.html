Executive summary

Across the first three days of the new Sun–Sat week (Sunday and Monday complete, Tuesday ~14.5 hours in at pull time), Eaze Account 2 has spent $2,196.03 to generate 28 appointments and 11 leads. RPF 01 remains the account's clear efficiency standout at $14.74/lead. RELAUNCH is the cheaper appointment engine at $64.66/appointment (18 appointments), while Aashish Maru [Do Not Touch] sits at $87.01/appointment (10 appointments) as its flagship ad, UGC Reel Ad 05, keeps spending without adding new bookings. Instagram remains the account's biggest and least efficient channel by a wide margin (62.3% of spend, 0.77% CTR vs. Facebook's 3.42% CTR). Tuesday's per-day numbers are visibly pricier across all three campaigns than Sunday's or Monday's, which is the expected shape of a still-settling partial day under Meta's attribution lag, not a real efficiency drop. The opportunity score holds flat at 76.

  • Notable change: Opportunity score held flat at 76; "Eaze Consulting Pixel1" PageView match quality improved (3.0 → 6.1) and the main "Eaze Consulting" pixel returned a Purchase event for the first time (8.8).
