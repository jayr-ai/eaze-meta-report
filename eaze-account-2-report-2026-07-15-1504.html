Executive summary

Across the first four days of the new Sun–Sat week (Sunday through Tuesday complete, Wednesday roughly 15 hours in), Eaze Account 2 has spent $3,044.37 to generate 43 appointments and 12 leads. Aashish Maru [Do Not Touch] remains the account's cheaper appointment engine for a second consecutive pull, $61.81/appointment (19 appointments) versus RELAUNCH's $68.58/appointment (24 appointments) — RELAUNCH still delivers more raw volume but at a higher unit cost. New Leads ad / RPF 01 remains the account's only lead engine at $18.68/lead, up from $16.29 last pull as the newest lead landed at a higher marginal cost. Instagram remains the account's largest platform by spend (58.7%) and its least efficient (0.79% CTR vs Facebook's 3.34% CTR), though its share of spend eased slightly from 61.5% last pull. The opportunity score recovered to 73 from 71, its first improvement since the drop flagged last pull.

  • Biggest drain: Instagram as a platform — 58.7% of spend at less than a quarter of Facebook's CTR.
