Executive summary

Across the first five days of the new Sun–Sat week (Sunday through Wednesday complete, Thursday only minutes old), Eaze Account 2 has spent $3,318.08 to generate 46 appointments and 12 leads. Aashish Maru [Do Not Touch] widened its lead as the account's cheaper appointment engine this pull, $60.01/appointment (21 appointments) versus RELAUNCH's $72.86/appointment (25 appointments) — the cost gap between the two engines grew from $6.77 to $12.85. New Leads ad / RPF 01 remains the account's only lead engine, but its cost per lead rose again to $19.71 (from $18.68) on flat lead volume — a real efficiency decline, not noise. Instagram remains the account's largest platform by spend (57.97%, down from 58.7%) and its least efficient (0.80% CTR vs Facebook's 3.24% CTR). The opportunity score reversed its recovery, dropping 73 → 70, as the AI-creative-variety recommendation grew from 3 to 5 flagged ads.

  • Best appointment engine: Aashish Maru [Do Not Touch] | Medspa UGC Reels — $60.01 per appointment, 21 appointments, 1.56% CTR — its cost advantage over RELAUNCH nearly doubled this pull.
  • Best lead engine: New Leads ad / RPF 01 - Dentists - WV - Images — $19.71 per lead, 12 leads, 4.59% CTR — the account's only lead-generation engine, but getting more expensive.
  • Biggest drain: Instagram as a platform — 57.97% of spend at less than a quarter of Facebook's CTR.
  • Notable change: Opportunity score dropped 73 → 70 as the AI-creative-variety recommendation expanded from 3 to 5 ads; a new, not-yet-firing dataset ("AI Funding - HVAC") appeared in the account overnight.
