Executive summary

Day 1 of the new Sun–Sat week (roughly the first 15 hours of Sunday, Jul 19), Eaze Account 2 has spent $457.19 to generate 6 appointments and 3 leads. Aashish Maru [Do Not Touch] is carrying the appointment side alone this early — $32.67/appointment, 6 appointments — while RELAUNCH, the account's other appointment campaign, has spent $216.60 (its single biggest campaign spend of the day) with zero recorded appointments so far, a floor number that should firm up as the day settles. Instagram is both the largest platform by spend (53.9% of platform spend) and the least efficient (1.81% CTR vs Facebook's 2.64%), continuing the pattern from prior reports.   • Best appointment engine: Aashish Maru [Do Not Touch] | Medspa UGC Reels — $32.67 per appointment, 6 appointments, 2.02% CTR, driven almost entirely by UGC Reel Ad 03 ($146.65 spend, 6 results, $24.44/result).

  • Biggest drain: RELAUNCH campaign — $216.60 spent (47.4% of the day's spend) with 0 recorded appointments so far; a floor figure given attribution lag, but the widest gap between spend and results in the account today.
  • Notable change: Opportunity score jumped 70 → 74 (+4), reversing the prior pull's decline; the fragmentation recommendation (combine two similar ad sets) is now the single largest lever on the table at 16 points.

Data & actions12

Action list

  1. Combine RELAUNCH's two fragmented ad sets — the single largest lever on the table (16 of 27 available points). They share setup and audience but differ only on creative, which is splitting learning-phase budget for no benefit.
  2. Investigate RELAUNCH's zero-appointment day — $216.60 spent (47% of today's budget) with 0 recorded appointments, while Aashish Maru converts on a third of the spend. Even accounting for attribution lag, this is the widest spend-to-result gap in the account today.
  3. Don't touch Aashish Maru / UGC Reel Ad 03 — carrying the entire appointment engine today at $24.44/result, 2.11% CTR. Protect its budget.
  4. Escalate "Eaze Consulting" PageView's softening fbc coverage (100% historically → 78.4% now) to whoever owns the pixel implementation — it hasn't broken yet, but it's trending the wrong way.
  5. Confirm "AI Funding - HVAC" dataset's purpose before it starts firing — still brand new (created Jul 15) with no usable match-quality data; verify manually in Events Manager whether it's intentional or a leftover from setup.
  6. Shift weight toward Android and away from over-indexing on iPhone where practical — Android smartphone runs at less than half of iPhone's CPC ($3.17 vs $7.33) with a much better CTR (3.27% vs 1.89%).
  7. Lean into the 55+ / female-skewing audience — 65+ has the best CTR in the account (3.97%), 55-64 is close behind (2.69%) at a reasonable CPC, and women outperform men on both CTR and CPC despite drawing less spend.
  8. Pull the plug on the 3 near-dead ads (UGC Reel Ad 01, V1 | AI Image 1 | Dentist, UGC Reel Ad 02) — $6.24 combined spend, zero results, one of them with a single impression all day.

Overall direction: a strong opening day for the new week — appointment cost efficiency and the opportunity score both improved — but RELAUNCH's stalled appointments and the still-unresolved ad-set fragmentation are the two things worth watching as the week fills in.
